
Sites — a site has:

A name/number
A category/type (linked to Categories)
Multiple photos
Multiple prices (price can vary by date range)
Full CRUD for Sites — Create, Read, Update, Delete

Photos — each site can have multiple photos uploaded

Prices — each site can have multiple prices tied to a date range (e.g. $50/night from June-August, $30/night Sept-May)

The workflow they're describing looks like:

List of sites → click a site → Edit site details
From the site edit page → click "Manage Photos" → add/remove photos
From the site edit page → click "Manage Prices" → add/remove price ranges
Navigate back to site list
So your database tables would be:

Sites (Id, Name, CategoryId, Description, etc.)
SitePhotos (Id, SiteId, PhotoUrl/FileName)
SitePrices (Id, SiteId, Price, StartDate, EndDate)
